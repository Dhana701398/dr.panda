2024

A Journey of Dedication

Hailing from a modest background, Dr. Ashwin Kumar's journey to becoming a cardiac
expert started at Andhra Medical College, Visakhapatnam, where he earned his MBBS. His
pursuit of excellence led him to complete his Post Graduation in Internal Medicine at
Maharaja Agrasen Hospital , New Delhi. Noteworthy stints followed as a Senior Resident in
the Department of Medicine at KGH and as a Senior Registrar in Cardiology at KIMS - ICON
Hospital.

A Quest for Expertise

As an Assistant Professor at NIMS, Hyderabad, and KGH, Visakhapatnam. Dr. Kumar
imparted knowledge to aspiring cardiologists and continually honed his craft. His academic
contributions and clinical acumen were pivotal in shaping the future of cardiac care. He
strived to improve cardiovascular health outcomes through rigorous research, innovative
treatments, and compassionate patient interactions.

Compassionate Care in Practice

Currently, Dr. Ashwin Kumar serves as a Consultant Interventional Cardiologist at Medicover,
Visakhapatnam, where he is known for his expertise in advanced cardiac procedures and
patient-focused care. His exceptional skills in interventional cardiology, coupled with his
compassionate approach, have earned him a stellar reputation among patients and peers
alike.


In addition to his primary role, Dr. Kumar extends his expertise as a Visiting Consultant at HAL Hospital, Sunabeda, where he provides specialized cardiac care and collaborates with a multidisciplinary team to enhance patient outcomes. His commitment to delivering high-quality, evidence-based treatments underscores his dedication to the field of cardiology.
